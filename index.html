About Us

Located in the town of Poras, Boac, Marinduque, AML's Resort offers a peaceful escape for travelers seeking relaxation in a serene natural setting.

As a family-owned establishment, we pride ourselves on providing guests with a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Our journey began with a passion for hospitality and a desire to create a retreat where guests can unwind and connect with nature.

At AML's Resort, our mission is simple: to ensure that every guest enjoys a memorable stay filled with comfort, relaxation, and personalized service. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or a longer vacation, we strive to exceed your expectations and create lasting memories.
